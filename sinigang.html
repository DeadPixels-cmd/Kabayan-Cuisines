Sinigang

(3 - 5 servings)

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. Pork belly, cubed
  • 66 g. Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix with Gabi
  • 2 pieces talong, sliced
  • 8 pieces okra
  • 18 pieces string beans, cut in 2-inch pieces
  • 5 ounces daikon radish, (labanos) sliced
  • 1 bunch kangkong
  • 1 piece onion, wedged
  • 2 pieces tomato, wedged
  • 6 pieces shishito pepper
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 8 cups water Fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste
Instructions
1
Heat oil in a cooking pot. Saute onion until layers separate. Add half of the tomato. Saute for 2 minutes. Saute garlic and onion.
2
Add pork belly. Continue sauteeing until the pork browns while adding around 1 tablespoon fish sauce.
3
Pour water. Cover the pot and let the liquid boil.
4
Add Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc with Gabi. Cover and adjust heat between low to medium.
5
Cook for 30 minutes or until the pork gets tender.
6
Add labanos. Cover and continue cooking for 30 minutes. Add eggplant, string beans, and okra. Cook for 5 minutes.
7
Add kangkong stalks and remaining tomato. Cook for 3 minutes. Season with fish sauce (as needed) and ground black pepper. Add kangkong leaves.
8
Turn the heat off. Cover and let it stay for 2 minutes. Transfer to a serving bowl
