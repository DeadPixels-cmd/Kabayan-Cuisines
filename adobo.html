Adobo

(One serving)

Ingredients

  • 1Kilo sliced pork liempo
  • 1/2Kilo sliced pork kasim (pork shoulder)
  • 3pcs chopped onions
  • 1 head chopped garlic
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 4Tbsp soy sauce
  • 3Tbsp vinegar
  • 1pc dried laurel
  • oil for pan frying.
Instructions
1
In a saucepan, put pork, vinegar, 1 tbsp. garlic, bay leaf, soy sauce, salt and pepper.
2
Bring to a boil then simmer uncovered until pork is tender. If it dries up then add water.
3
Remove meat from sauce. In a skillet, heat oil then saut√© remaining garlic.
4
Add meat and fry until light brown.
5
Add back the sauce.
6
Remove from heat and serve.
