Sisig

(One serving)

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lbs. pork belly, boiled for 1 hour
  • ½ cup Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise
  • 2 pieces onion, chopped
  • 4 pieces Thai chili pepper, chopped
  • 2 pieces long green pepper, chopped
  • 16 grams Knorr SavorRich Pork
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • 2 cups cooking oil
Instructions
1
Heat oil in a deep cooking pot or deep fryer.
2
Fry the boiled pork belly until golden brown and crispy. Chop the crispy pork belly into small pieces. Set aside.
3
Heat half of the margarine in a pan. Sauté half of the onions until soft. Add chopped pork. Saute for 30 seconds.
4
Add Knorr SavorRich. Stir and then add chili peppers and season with ground black pepper and soy sauce. Cook for 1 minute.
5
Add Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise. Stir and add remaining onion. Set aside. Heat a metal plate (sizzling plate) on a stovetop.
6
Melt remaining margarine. Transfer the crispy pork sisig into the metal plate. Let it stay for 30 seconds.
7
Serve with warm rice and your favorite beverage. Enjoy!
